FINANCE TRACKER

Elective 2

This is a Finance Tracker mobile application, developed using Android Studio with Dart code. The app helps users monitor their income and expenses efficiently. It features a clean interface displaying a total balance at the top, categorized income (green) and expenses (red) with icons, descriptions, and amounts. Users can add new transactions with a "+" button, ensuring organized and streamlined financial management.
